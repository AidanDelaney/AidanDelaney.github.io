Computational Thinking

Elements

Decomposition
Breaking down data, processes, or problems into smaller, manageable parts
Pattern Recognition
Observing patterns, trends, and regularities in data
Abstraction
Identifying the general principles that generate these patterns
Algorithm Design
Developing the step by step instructions for solving this and similar problems

Google's presentation is a good introduction to Computational Thinking.

Concept Application

CT Concept CT Application
Decomposition Literature: Break down the analysis of a poem into analysis of meter, rhyme, imagery, structure, tone, diction, and meaning.
Pattern Recognition Economics: Find cycle patterns in the rise and drop of the country's economy.
Abstraction Culinary Arts: Write a recipe for others to use.
Algorithm Design Chemistry: Determine the rules for chemical bonding and interactions.
